// FinEval 2026 Shared Task

Multilingual Financial Language Understanding

FinNLP-2026 @ EMNLP-2026 · October 24–29, 2026 · Budapest, Hungary

Register Official task page

Overview

This FinEval 2026 shared task advances multilingual financial language understanding through three complementary phenomena: entity extraction in Greek, pragmatic intent recognition in Japanese, and cross-document multilingual financial question answering.

Participants may take part in one, two, or all three subtasks. System description papers will be published in the ACL Anthology and presented at FinNLP @ EMNLP 2026.

Subtasks

// Subtask 1

Greek Financial NER

Numeric and textual named-entity recognition over company filings from Athens Stock Exchange firms.

Data: GRFinNUM & GRFinNER 500 instances (320 / 80 / 100) Metric: Entity F1
// Subtask 2

Japanese Implicit Communication Recognition (JF-ICR)

Classify implicit intent — such as commitment, non-commitment, risk signaling, or refusal — in Japanese company Q&A.

94 expert-annotated JP Q&A instances Metric: Accuracy
// Subtask 3

Multilingual Financial Q&A

Cross-document multilingual financial QA combining SEC filing statements with news, based on MultiFinBen / PolyFiQA.

Data: PolyFiQA-Easy & PolyFiQA-Expert 172 QA examples EN · ZH · JA · ES · EL Metric: ROUGE-1
